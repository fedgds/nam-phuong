Information

As a restaurant cashier, you will handle customer transactions, process payments, and provide accurate change. You ensure smooth operations by taking orders, issuing receipts, and answering any payment-related inquiries. Cashiers may also assist with balancing the cash register, managing credit card payments, and maintaining a clean checkout area. Strong customer service skills and attention to detail are essential for this role.

Responsibilities

  • Manage transactions with customers using cash registers
  • Scan goods and ensure pricing is accurate
  • Collect payments whether in cash or credit
  • Issue receipts, refunds, change, or tickets
  • Redeem stamps and vouchers
  • Cross-sell products and introduce new ones
  • Maintain clean and tidy checkout areas
  • Track transactions on balance sheets and report any discrepancies
  • Handle merchandise returns and exchanges
